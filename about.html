// MY TAKE ON DEVELOPMENT

My biggest takeaway from uni so far? An AI model is only 20% of a good software product. The other 80% is making sure the data is clean, the AI isn't making things up, and the interface it lives inside actually feels fast, friendly, and enjoyable for a human to click around in.
